About us

CRC Maesta

Sector 1, Greater Noida

Nestled in the prestigious neighbourhood of Greater Noida West, Sector 1, Maesta stands as a beacon of premium living. Spanning across approx 4 acres, this landmark project offers an unparalleled lifestyle just 0 km from Noida. With 3.5 acres dedicated to lush greenery, residents can immerse themselves in nature every day.

Maesta is designed to provide a truly exceptional lifestyle, featuring a state-of-the-art clubhouse equipped with exclusive lifestyle and wellness amenities. Whether you seek relaxation or recreation, Maesta has it all. The project offers modern and premium 3 & 3.5 BHK apartments, combining elegance with comfort.
